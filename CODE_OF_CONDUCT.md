# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Be respectful, constructive and specific. Welcome questions asked in good faith. Focus criticism on code, evidence and behavior rather than people. Do not publish another person's credentials, private data or financial account information.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption and disclosure of private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct issues privately to `yilugesanhua@gmail.com`. The repository owner may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions and other project spaces.
